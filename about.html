About Us

Five Stars Car Rental was founded in 2010 with the mission to provide high-quality vehicles at affordable prices. We started with just 5 cars and have grown to a fleet of over 50 vehicles serving customers across the country.

Our team consists of passionate car enthusiasts who understand what customers look for in a rental experience. We meticulously maintain our vehicles to ensure they're always in top condition when you drive them.

What We Do

We specialize in short-term and long-term rentals of premium vehicles. Whether you need a car for a business trip, vacation, or special occasion, we have the perfect vehicle for your needs.
